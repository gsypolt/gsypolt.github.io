About Greg

As a VP of Quality Assurance at EverFi, Greg Sypolt speaks and writes about how others can rethink how they approach quality excellence. He has spent most of this career in quality assurance with an engineering mindset, gaining experience in tool development, automated testing, and DevOps. In the last five years, he successfully transitioned Gannett from a traditional manual testing strategy, coaching multiple development teams to build quality into every continuous integration pipeline stage. In 2020, Greg joined EverFi to create a culture of quality excellence and transition to an automation mindset like Gannett's success. Quality is a journey, not a destination. Greg is building a culture of software-quality excellence in which every member of the organization shares quality responsibilities. The mindset is clear, and it requires a culture that embraces quality in every stage of the development lifecycle. His focus is being the voice of quality from the start of the intake process, not at the end, ensuring development teams are building quality software that customers love. Outside of his role at EverFi, Greg is passionate about do-it-yourself projects around the house and recently single-handedly finished his basement himself. If you're looking for someone to talk to about quality excellence, reach out to Greg on Twitter or LinkedIn to geek out about testing, technology, or do-it-yourself projects.
